New Office Inauguration Malta

The Event

Welcome to the information page for our new office inauguration in Malta. Here you will find all the details about what we have planned for this special day, from the agenda and entertainment to the specifics of the space.

  • Welcome & Networking: A moment to meet colleagues and celebrate this new milestone.
  • Official Speeches: Words from our leadership about the future and the importance of this new location.
  • The Official Launch: The symbolic moment marking the official start of our operations. A button will be pushed, triggering a special inaugural video.
  • Food & Beverages: Enjoy high-quality catering throughout the event.
  • Live Music: We will have a DJ, violinist, and saxophonist to ensure an incredible atmosphere.

Entertainment & Music Schedule

Our A/V setup will provide a vibrant atmosphere throughout the inauguration:

  • 09:00 - 10:00: Background Music
  • 10:00 - 11:00: Violin and DJ Duo
  • 11:00 - 12:00: Saxophone and DJ Duo
  • 12:00 - 13:00: Violin, Saxophone and DJ Trio
  • 13:00 - 15:00: DJ Solo Session
